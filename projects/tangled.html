Tangled (Startup, 2020 - Present)

Objective

To create a peer-learning platform for students aged 7-21, enabling them to learn from teenage instructors.

Technologies & Skills

  • Platform: Custom-built learning management system with gamification via Tangled Tokens
  • Business Development: Recruited 50 instructors and grew the learner base to 1000+ students

Impact & Recognition

  • Community: Successfully organized the Tanglethon with 221 participants
  • Growth: Built a thriving community of teenage instructors and learners
