Workshops & Educational Initiatives

Overview

A collection of workshops and educational initiatives I've conducted to share knowledge and inspire others in technology.

MiO STEM Classes (2020)

  • Impact: Taught 120 students for 8 weeks, scaled to 800+ students in Oman
  • Content: Robotics, circuits, and microcontroller programming
  • Age Group: Students aged 8-16

Hunger for Knowledge (2019)

  • Objective: Provide students in under-resourced areas of India and Bhutan with exposure to emerging technologies
  • Activities: Organized live tours of trade shows in Singapore using virtual presence robots
  • Recognition: Delivered the closing plenary at SMF 2019 (largest MICE forum in Southeast Asia)

  • Mentoring sessions for aspiring young entrepreneurs
